# TAU School of Electrical Engineering Thesis Guidelines

## Master's Thesis

### 1. Purpose of the Thesis

- To provide students with experience in conducting research, analyzing literature, and writing scientific reports.
- To enable students to prove a hypothesis and demonstrate independent capability in summarizing and processing scientific material.
- The thesis should reflect a research period of two years.

### 2. Thesis Format

The thesis must include the following sections, in order:

1. English cover page (see example provided by the school)
2. Table of contents
3. List of abbreviations
4. Abstract in Hebrew (up to 2 pages; covering research goals, methods, results, and conclusions)
5. Keywords (5–10)
6. Introduction (up to 10 pages; issue, problem, literature review)
7. Study goals and secondary goals (if any)
8. Research methods (materials, equipment, methods)
9. Results (narrative, tables, figures; avoid repetition)
10. Discussion and conclusions (do not repeat introduction or results)
11. List of references (numbered uniformly)
12. Appendices
13. Abstract in Hebrew (up to 2 pages)
14. Hebrew cover page (for back binding; same format as English cover)
